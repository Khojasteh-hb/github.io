Research Positions

2018–2019      University of Zanjan, Department of Bioinformatics, Graduate Research  

Assistant under direction of Alireza Khanteymoori. Dissertation and thesis work. Working closely with the lab mates on various machine learning techniques (supervised and unsupervised) for link prediction. Studying various machine learning techniques to predict new interactions in complex networks. Analyzing complex networks topologically in different applications and consequently taking good ideas for applying in biological networks like Gene Regulatory networks. Studying Content-based node proximity features for effective feature extraction in biological networks and as a result, extracting 3 relative features for use along with the original dataset in the training phase. Studying innovative methods in Data science and handling imbalanced data in order to deal with imbalanced data classiﬁcation in biological networks like Gene Regulatory networks.  

2020–2021      University of Zanjan, Department of Bioinformatics, Assistant    

Assistant under direction of Alireza Khanteymoori and Mohammad Hossein Olyaee. Studying SARS-CoV-2 and (H1N1) influenza PPINs to investigate similarities and differences between them.  Examining complex network properties such as graph density, degree distribution, and 21 different centrality measures. Fitting power law and exponential distributions. Dimensionality reduction and Applying unsupervised learning methods to reveal appropriate centrality measures.  

2020–present       University of Zanjan, Department of Bioinformatics, Assistant    

 Assistant under direction of Jamshid Pirgazi. Studying and implementing a new approach for Predicting drug-target interactions. Preprocessing, Selecting appropriate features among of (Frequency of Amino acids in protein sequences, PSSM, fingerprints of drug compounds, and so on). Applying an innovative machine learning approach in the drug-target interaction network for increasing accuracy in prediction
